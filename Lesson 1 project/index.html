Lesson 1 - The Powerpuff Girls

Bubbles

Bubbles is the fun and bubbly one of the three hence her name, Bubbles. She is cute but fierce when is comes to fighting bad guys.

Blossom

Blossom is the redhead and the leader of the group. She keeps them in check and makes sure that they work well together.

Buttercup

Buttercup is the tomboy of the group in the sense that her actions are boyish like.
